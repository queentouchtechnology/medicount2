Ohio Attorney General Debt Collection

The Ohio Attorney General’s office will attempt to collect an EMS Agency’s unpaid transport bills that are the patient’s responsibility.; after Medicount has exhausted all means of payment recovery. If you are interested in using this service, contact the Ohio Attorney General Collection Enforcement Agency at (614) 466-4510.
